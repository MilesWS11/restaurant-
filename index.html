History of the Pub

The Stokie Pub was originally founded in 1863 the same year Stoke City Football Club was made and was a popular spot even then to have a meal before going to the match. It was very popular with factoty workers and railroad workers and going in to have a pint in the evening before going home. During World War 2 the bar was destroyed due to bombing and was rebuilt in the mid 50s and picked up buisness where it had left off .Over the years it has become a must go place to eat in Stoke-on-Trent and has become a match day essential.
